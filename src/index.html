Our Story

Built by an RVer, for RVers

I’m not just another RV tech—I’m an RVer, just like you. Since 2018, I’ve been living full-time in a 5th wheel and a truck camper, so I understand firsthand how frustrating it is when something stops working and you're far from a dealership or stuck waiting for a callback that never comes.

That’s why I started AAA RV Tech—to offer a better way. I’m an Advanced Certified RV Technician who brings the repair shop to you, whether you’re parked in a driveway, a campground, or somewhere off-grid near Portland, Oregon. No towing, no long waits, just real help when you need it.

From fixing fridges and furnaces to solving tank issues, leaky roofs, and more, I handle the stuff that keeps your RV running—and your trip on track. I care about doing the job right, treating your rig with respect, and getting you back to enjoying the RV life as fast as possible.
