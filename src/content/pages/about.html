About AAA RV Tech

Built by an RVer. Run by an RVer. For RVers.

Hey, I’m the face behind AAA RV Tech—and I’ve been living the RV life full-time since 2018. That means I know exactly what it’s like when something breaks in your rig. Whether it’s your fridge going warm, your roof springing a leak, or your tanks causing trouble, I’ve been there. I’ve dealt with it. And now, I help others get through it—quickly and stress-free.

Why I started AAA RV Tech

After years on the road and more than a few frustrating service experiences, I decided to do something about it. I trained, got certified, and built this business to offer what I wished existed: mobile, honest, professional RV repair that actually comes to you.

As an Advanced Certified RV Technician, I’m trained to handle everything from appliance repairs to plumbing, solar installs, roof work, and more. But beyond the skills and certifications, I bring something else to every job—real empathy. I treat every RV like it’s my own, because I know how important it is to keep your home on wheels safe, functional, and road-ready.

What you can expect

  • I show up when I say I will.
    No endless waiting, no dealership runaround. Just real service, on your schedule.
  • I fix it right, the first time.
    Whether it's a quick tune-up or a bigger repair, I take pride in quality work and clear communication.
  • No upsells, no fluff.
    I won’t talk you into things you don’t need. I’ll tell you what’s wrong, what it’ll take to fix it, and get to work.
